The coal used in a coal powered plant has carbon along with large quantities of sulfur and produces CO2, CO, SO2, SO3, NO2 etc. These gases are called greenhouse gases and lead to global warming and environmental pollution. Hydro plants are environment friendly which however is achieved at the cost of large areas of fertile land being submerged in water.
